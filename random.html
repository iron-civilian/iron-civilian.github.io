Shob-E-Maya went on a trip to Assam and Meghalaya in April, 2024 before graduating from IISER-Kolkata. We trekked to the iconic Double Decker Root Bridge, camped by the serene Dawki riverside (Wah Umngot), and savored the delicious local Khasi cuisine. Apart from a few unprecedented incidents (hitting an army vehicle and nearly driving off a cliff) the trip was amazing!
