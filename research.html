We study motility-induced phase separation behavior of RTPs in 2D. We look at the percolation properties of clusters formed and characterize the critical behavior. We obeserve a continuous variation in exponents which we explain through the Super-Universality Hypothesis. Soft Matter, 2024.

We study the effect of non-reciprocity in the classical q-state Potts Model. We show that the added non-reciprocity keeps the Potts Universality intact for the equilibrium dynamics. We obeserve a continuous variation in exponents in the non-equilibrium case which we explain through the Super-Universality Hypothesis.
