We study motility-induced phase separation behavior of RTPs in 2D. We look at the percolation properties of clusters formed and characterize the critical behavior. We obeserve a continuous variation in exponents which we explain through the Super-Universality Hypothesis. Soft Matter, 2024.

We study the effect of non-reciprocity in the classical Ising Model. We modify the ordinary Ising model to incorporate non-reciprocal interactions. We show that despite the novel phase behavior induced by non-reciprocity, it fails to modify the critical behavior of the magnetization transition.
